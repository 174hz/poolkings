Official Contest Rules

These Official Rules (“Rules”) apply to all contests (“Pools”) offered by PoolKings. By entering any Pool, you agree to these Rules, the Terms of Use, and the Privacy Policy.

1. No Purchase Necessary

All Pools are free to enter. No purchase or payment is required to participate or win.

2. Eligibility

  • Open to legal residents of Canada aged 18 or older.
  • Void where prohibited by law.
  • Employees of PoolKings may be restricted from entering certain Pools.

3. How to Enter

  • Create a PoolKings account.
  • Select a Pool and submit your predictions before the posted deadline.
  • Only one entry per person per Pool unless otherwise stated.

4. Scoring & Determining Winners

  • Each Pool uses a skill‑based scoring system based on prediction accuracy.
  • Scores are calculated using publicly available sports results.
  • In the event of a tie, PoolKings may use timestamp, additional criteria, or a skill‑testing question.

5. Skill‑Testing Question

To comply with Canadian contest law, potential winners must correctly answer a mathematical skill‑testing question without assistance.

6. Prizes

  • Prizes are non‑transferable and may not be substituted.
  • Winners will be notified via email.
  • PoolKings may require identity verification before awarding prizes.

7. Disqualification

PoolKings may disqualify entries for:

  • Late submissions
  • Multiple entries
  • Use of bots or automated tools
  • Fraudulent or suspicious activity

8. Publicity

By entering, you consent to the use of your name and general location for promotional purposes without additional compensation.

9. Liability

PoolKings is not responsible for technical failures, data loss, or errors in scoring or results.

10. Governing Law

These Rules are governed by the laws of Ontario, Canada.
