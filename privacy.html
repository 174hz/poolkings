Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

PoolKings (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a free, skill‑based sports prediction platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights available to you.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address (required for account creation, pool entry, and winner notifications)
  • Username or display name
  • Optional demographic information (if provided voluntarily)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Device type, browser type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, referral sources
  • Interaction data with ads and content

1.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Analytics and performance measurement
  • Advertising personalization and frequency capping

Third‑party ad partners (e.g., Google AdSense, Taboola) may also use cookies to deliver relevant ads and measure performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and administer free skill‑based pools
  • To verify eligibility and contact winners
  • To personalize user experience
  • To serve and optimize advertising
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Advertising partners (for ad delivery and measurement)
  • Analytics providers (aggregated, anonymized data)
  • Service providers assisting with hosting, security, or email delivery
  • Regulators or law enforcement when legally required

4. Advertising & Third‑Party Tracking

PoolKings uses third‑party ad networks that may collect anonymized data to deliver personalized ads. These partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and browsing data.

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or via the Digital Advertising Alliance.

5. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no online system is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal requirements, and maintain accurate contest records.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for marketing or tracking
  • Request a copy of your data

Contact: privacy@poolkings.com

8. International Compliance

PoolKings complies with PIPEDA (Canada), GDPR (EU), and CCPA (California) where applicable.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
